This page collects common student questions during setup, experiments, and submission, using the legacy student FAQ as source material but updating the language around current versions.

## Which version should I use?

Use the version assigned by your instructor. If you were not told which one to use, start with [Choose a Version](../get-started/choose-version.md).

## Will Avida-ED run on my computer?

The safest current guidance is:

- use a modern desktop or laptop browser
- follow [System Requirements](../get-started/system-requirements.md)
- use Avida-ED 4 by default unless your instructor requires a legacy workflow

The older FAQ included browser and device notes specific to the Avida-ED 3 beta era. Those details are no longer reliable enough to repeat verbatim.

## How much does it cost?

Avida-ED is free.

## Are there instructions for how to use Avida-ED?

Yes. Start with:

- [Student First Steps](../students/first-steps.md)
- [Reports and Submission](../students/reports-and-submission.md)
- [Videos & Guides](../videos/index.md)

## What should I do first after launch?

Run one baseline experiment with the default settings unless your instructor gave different instructions.

## What if my results look different from someone else's?

That can happen. First confirm that you used the same settings and the same run length. Then compare what changed and what stayed the same before drawing conclusions.

## What if I cannot export or save my work?

Try again before closing the app. If it still fails, record exactly what you did, what you expected, and what happened, then tell your instructor. Use [Reports and Submission](../students/reports-and-submission.md) for the checklist.

## Where can I get another explanation of the task?

Use [Student First Steps](../students/first-steps.md) or the [student walkthroughs](../videos/student-walkthroughs.md).

## Can I change environmental settings during a run?

That depends on the version and the specific control. If your assignment depends on changing settings mid-run, follow the activity instructions carefully and ask your instructor to confirm the intended workflow for your version of Avida-ED.
