## What you can do with Avida-ED

Avida-ED supports instruction in:

- natural selection and adaptation
- mutation and variation
- experimental design and hypothesis testing
- interpreting data and scientific explanations

## Typical classroom formats

- **Single-period lab:** guided run + structured questions
- **Multi-day activity:** compare conditions across groups
- **Inquiry project:** student-designed experimental questions with reporting

## Build your teaching path

- Start with [Choose a Version](../get-started/choose-version.md) and use **Avida-ED 4** unless a legacy lesson specifically requires v3.
- Pull a ready-to-use activity from [Curriculum](../curriculum/index.md).
- Keep [Support](../support/index.md) open for troubleshooting and FAQs during class.
