---
title: Classroom Tips
---

These tips are intended to reduce avoidable classroom friction when using Avida-ED.

## Before class

- Test the exact device and browser combination students will use.
- Decide in advance whether the class will use Avida-ED 4 or legacy Avida-ED 3.
- Write down what students must submit before class begins.
- Keep a backup plan for students who need a rerun or export help.

## During class

- Start with one baseline run before changing settings.
- Require students to change only one variable at a time.
- Have groups record settings immediately so results are interpretable later.
- Pause for a short comparison discussion before students move to explanation.

## Support strategy

- Keep [Troubleshooting Checklist](../get-started/troubleshooting.md) open on the instructor machine.
- Direct students with routine problems to [Student FAQ](../support/student-faq.md).
- Use [Instructor FAQ](../support/instructor-faq.md) for planning and setup questions.

## Curriculum continuity tip

If you are reusing older lesson plans, verify whether they were written for Avida-ED 1, 2, or 3. The underlying concepts usually still transfer, but screenshots, names of controls, and some workflow details may not.
