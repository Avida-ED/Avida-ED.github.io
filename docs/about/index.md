---
title: About Avida-ED
---

Avida-ED is an educational platform for learning about evolution and the nature of science using digital evolution experiments.

## What Avida-ED is for

Avida-ED supports instruction and learning in:

- evolutionary mechanisms (variation, inheritance, selection)
- experimental design and scientific reasoning
- data interpretation and scientific explanation
