Avida-ED supports evolution education by letting learners investigate change in digital populations using experimental comparisons and evidence-based explanation.

## Educational focus

Avida-ED is designed to help learners work with:

- variation, inheritance, and selection
- adaptation and population change
- experimental design
- interpretation of evidence
- the nature of scientific explanation

- short background summaries for instructors

## Legacy background themes to preserve

The legacy digital-evolution and about pages emphasized several recurring themes:

- Avida-ED is for learning both **evolution** and the **nature of science**
- digital organisms support experimental comparison and evidence-based reasoning
- the software is connected to a longer-running digital evolution research platform
- the project has both curricular and research publications that matter for instructor trust

## Framing note

One distinctive argument from the legacy site is worth preserving carefully: Avida-ED was described not merely as a simulation of evolution, but as an instance of digital evolution. That claim should remain part of the research/background section rather than being placed in basic getting-started content.
