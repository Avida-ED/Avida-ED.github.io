Avida-ED supports evolution education by letting learners investigate change in digital populations using experimental comparisons and evidence-based explanation.

## Educational focus

Avida-ED is designed to help learners work with:

- variation, inheritance, and selection
- adaptation and population change
- experimental design
- interpretation of evidence
- the nature of scientific explanation
