---
title: "Artificial Selection: Evolution in Practice"
---

Rett Weber, Wendy Johnson & Amy Lark

Students are presented with a hypothetical scenario in which they use the
mechanism of artificial selection to evolve an organism with a particular
trait.

## Key concepts

- Selection  
- Adaptation  
- Experimental evolution

## Recommended use

Use this lesson when you want students to connect selective pressures to
intentional trait choice in a concrete scenario.

## What students do

- decide which traits to favor in a selective scenario  
- evaluate how repeated selection changes outcomes  
- connect artificial selection to broader evolutionary reasoning
