---
title: Reports and Submission
---

Use this page when your assignment requires you to turn observations from Avida-ED into something you can submit.

## A strong submission usually includes

- the version of Avida-ED you used
- the important settings or condition you changed
- what you observed
- evidence from the run, export, or figure
- an explanation of why the result happened

## Before submitting

- Confirm the file downloaded correctly if you exported data.
- Use your instructor's required file name format.
- Make sure screenshots include labels or captions if the assignment needs them.
- Check that your explanation refers to evidence, not just opinion.

## If something goes wrong

- Retry the export before closing the app.
- If a file is missing, note what you did and tell your instructor exactly where the process failed.
- Use the [Troubleshooting Checklist](../get-started/troubleshooting.md) and [Student FAQ](../support/student-faq.md).
