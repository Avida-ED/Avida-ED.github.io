---
title: Exploring the Effects of Mutation Rate on Individuals
---

Amy Lark

This exercise is based on a 2012 study that examined butterflies in the
vicinity of the damaged Fukushima nuclear power plant that were exposed to
leaked radiation. Students test the study's conclusion that increased mutation
rate likely caused the adverse biological effects. An article on this model
lesson was published in *American Biology Teacher*.

## Key concepts

- Mutation  
- Fitness  
- Individual variation

## Recommended use

Use this lesson when you want students to evaluate a real-world-inspired claim
about mutation rate and connect that claim to evidence from a controlled digital
environment.

## What students do

- test the effects of mutation-rate changes on digital organisms  
- compare evidence from the digital system with a real-world claim  
- discuss what the digital evidence can and cannot support
