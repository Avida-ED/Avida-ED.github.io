title: Experimental Evolution Project with Evolving Digital Organisms
---

Robert T. Pennock & Amy Lark

This assignment supports an open-ended major project. Students propose an
evolutionary hypothesis that can be tested with Avida-ED, design and conduct an
experiment, analyze their data, and write up a research report.

## Recommended use

Use this after students have already completed guided work with the interface and
with evidence-based comparison across runs.

## Teaching emphasis

- question formation  
- experimental design  
- data interpretation  
- scientific writing or presentation

## What students do

- define a testable evolutionary question  
- choose variables and controls for an experiment  
- analyze results from repeated runs  
- present a written or oral evidence-based conclusion
