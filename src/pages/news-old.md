This page preserves historical announcements about Avida-ED.

Archived updates belong here instead of the main teaching pathways.

## Archive status

- Earlier announcements and release notes remain available for reference.
- Time-sensitive guidance belongs in current support or curriculum pages.
- Historical milestones remain discoverable without dominating the main
  teaching workflow.

## What kinds of items belong here

The legacy site used this section for items such as:

- workshop and conference announcements
- project milestones and release-era notes
- historical news about outreach, awards, and presentations

Those materials remain useful for project history, but they should not be the
main route for a student trying to launch the app or an instructor trying to
prepare a lesson.

## Where current readers should usually go instead

- [Launch Avida-ED](/app)
- [For Students](/docs/students)
- [For Instructors](/curriculum)
- [Help](/help)
- [Vintage Archive](/about/vintage-archive)

## Migration note

When older news items are reviewed, each should either:

- remain here as archival context
- be condensed into a historical summary page
- be replaced by a current support or curriculum page if the information is
  still operationally important
